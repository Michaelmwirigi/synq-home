SOLUTIONS

What defines us

SMS + VOICE

We work with the world leaders in SMS and voice communication solutions across Africa and the Middle East. This makes it possible for enterprises, developers and other OTT customers to send and receive global SMS, MMS, and IP messaging from any app, and ensure deliverability using intelligent delivery features.

AIRTIME TOP UP

Solutions that enable mobile operators to dispense prepaid products and services such as airtime direct top-up which allows the subscriber to simply provide their phone number and the merchant can add the funds at the time of the transaction.

CONTENT + VAS

Cloud-based service delivery platform that allows mobile operators to rapidly deploy rich content offerings over WAP, USSD, App and SMS. Carriers can thus maximize content penetration in their subscriber base by including a richly populated USSD content menu on existing, busy customer service strings.

MOBILE SURVEYS + BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE

We are able to offer mobile operators tools to quickly measure their performance and make adjustments to campaigns that they launch. This can work across a range of products, services and communications sent out.
